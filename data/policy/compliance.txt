COMPLIANCE INFORMATION

GDPR Compliance:
- We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- EU users have specific rights regarding their personal data
- Data processing agreements available for business customers
- Right to data portability and deletion

SOC 2 Compliance:
- We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification
- Annual security audits conducted
- Access controls and monitoring in place
- Incident response procedures established

PCI DSS Compliance:
- Payment processing complies with PCI DSS standards
- Credit card data is not stored on our servers
- Secure payment gateway integration
- Regular security assessments

HIPAA Compliance:
- Healthcare customers can request HIPAA-compliant data handling
- Business Associate Agreements (BAA) available
- Enhanced security measures for healthcare data
- Audit trails and access logging

Data Residency:
- Data stored in region-specific data centers
- EU data stored in EU data centers
- US data stored in US data centers
- Custom data residency options for Enterprise customers

Audit and Reporting:
- Regular compliance audits conducted
- Compliance reports available upon request
- Transparency in data handling practices
- Incident reporting procedures

Legal Requirements:
- We comply with applicable data protection laws
- Regular legal review of practices
- Updates to policies as laws change
- Cooperation with regulatory authorities

Certifications:
